Surf's Up

the movie about surfing penguins

Surf's Up (2007) is a cinematic masterpiece and the pinnacle of filmmaking.

This seemingly absurd animated film about surfing birds may not be the first thing that comes to mind when you thinik of great art of the twenty-first century, but it is truly one of the greats, just beating out Penguins of Madagascar (2014).

Some of the cast includes:

  • Shia LeBeouf as Cody Maverik, penguin and star of the film
  • Jeff Bridges as Zeke, penguin and mentor
  • John Heder as Chicken Joe, chicken and surfer
  • Diedrich Bader as Tank Evans, penguin and reigning surfer champion

Mocumentary format

One of the most unique features of the movie is the style. The movie is framed as a documentary exploring the ins and outs of a surfing competition. This allows for there to several talking-head style interviews where characters share thier feelings about the events of the story. It is unclear whether the documentarians are also birds or just regular people making a movie about surfing penguins. It is also unclear if there are any human beings in the world at all. Perhaps they live thier regular lives, completely unaware of the intense rivalries and gnarly wipeouts of this bird-centric society. If they are, they are surely worse off.
